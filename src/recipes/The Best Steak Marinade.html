The Best Steak Marinade

    Ingredients
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1/4 cup balsamic vinegar
  • 1/4 cup Worcestershire sauce
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 teaspoons Dijon mustard
  • 2 teaspoons minced garlic
  • 1 pinch salt and pepper to taste
    Direction
  • Step 1: Mix olive oil, balsamic vinegar, Worcestershire sauce, soy sauce, Dijon mustard, and garlic in a small bowl. Season with salt and pepper.
  • Cook's Note: Marinate steaks for at least 2 hours for best flavor.
